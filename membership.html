About Membership

  • Any qualified member over the age of eighteen years shall beeligible for membership of the association and shall, subject to approval of the training and registration committee, becomes a member on payment of annual subscription fee of KSh 2000.

  • Every member shall pay a monthly welfare subscription ofKSh 100 not later than the 15th day of each month from thesecond month after payment of the entrance fee;

  • Any member who had paid any amount before the registration of this association with the Registrar of Societies, to the association in the name of registration fee, will only be required to top-up such amount to full entrance fee any time before the end of the subsequent month. Payment of registration fee is one of, but not the only requirement forrecogniton as registered member;

  • Members will be issued with membership card, and either professional practice card or provisional license;

  • Any member desiring to resign from the association shall submit his resignation to the registrar of POTTA Kenya, whichshall take effect from the date of receipt by the registrar of such notice.

  • Any member maybe expelled from membership if the trainingand registration committee so recommends and if a generalmeeting of the association shall resolve by a two-thirdsmajority of the members present that such a member should be expelled on grounds that his conduct has adversely affected thereputation or dignity of the society, or that he has contravenedany of the provisions of the constitution of the society.
